Mission Statement

Our mission is to focus on establishing a relationship between the students, the QU community and the local law enforcement to better understand our Criminal Justice System.

Why Join?

By joining us, not only will you have fun, but you also build relationships with many people. We hope that our students get the education they need to further the career for the future.
