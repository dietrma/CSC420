Requirements

Why Join?

By applying to Alpha Phi Sigma, you are giving yourself a great way to start your Criminal Justice career. This is a national organization that is held at high standards for the applicants that are involved. This is a great item to have on a resume when applying to Criminal Justice related jobs.
